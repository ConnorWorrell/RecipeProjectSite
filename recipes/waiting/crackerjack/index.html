Cracker Jack

Note: this recipe makes 1 gallon, not a single serving.

Ingredients

IngredientStandardStandard UnitWeight 1Unit 1Weight 2Unit 2
Molasses3Tbsp.
Butter1Tbsp.
Sugar0.5Cups
Salt0.25Tspn.
Vinegar (white)2Tbsp.
Baking Soda.25Tspn.
Popped Corn1Gal.

Objects

Object(Optional)
Sauce PanNo

Instructions

  1. Mix ingredients in sauce pan.
  2. Heat on high until mixture spins thread (230-234F).
  3. Remove from heat, add baking soda.
  4. Pour over popped corn, mix.

Eating Now

  1. Eat.

Prep for Eating Later

  1. Store.

Eating Later

  1. Eat.
