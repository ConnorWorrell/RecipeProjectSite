Franken Waffles

Object(Optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat waffle iron.
  2. Cook bacon on stove, drain grease.
  3. Seperate eggs into two bowls, whisk together.
    • 1 egg (whites + yolk) and 1 yolk
    • 1 egg whites
  4. Mix egg whites bowl, flour, milk, 0.025 Cup Butter (melted), sugar, baking pwd., salt, vanilla.
  5. Season other egg bowl with pepper.
  6. Spread thin coat of remaining butter over waffle iron between each batch.
  7. Pour batter mix into waffle iron in half of the quantiy listed in iron instructions, fill the other half of iron with egg mixture, lay bacon on top of egg mixture.
  8. After all batter is used, use remaining melted butter to clean iron.

Note: You may need to hold the waffle iron shut, the egg tends to expand and open the iron
