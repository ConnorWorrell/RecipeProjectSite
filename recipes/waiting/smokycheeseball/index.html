Smoky Cheese Ball

Note: this recipe is not in single serving format

Ingredients

IngredientStandardStandard UnitWeight 1Unit 1Weight 2Unit 2

Butter0.5Cups
Milk2Tbsp.
Steak Sauce2Tspn.

Objects

Object(Optional)

Instructions

  1. Finely chop toasted almonds.
  2. Mix ingridents, chill 15 min, form into ball, coat with toasted almonds.
