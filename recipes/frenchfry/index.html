Instructions

  1. Heat oil to 375F.
  2. Cut potatoes into slices, wedges or other.
  3. Dip cut potatoes into egg mixture, coat in flour mixture.
  4. Place in frier for 4 - 8 mins (very thin - very thick), drain, repeat for all potatoes.
  5. Refry potatoes for 2 - 3 mins (very thin - very thick), drain, repeat for all potatoes.
  6. Toss with salt, paprica, pepper, garlic salt.

Eating Now

  1. Eat.

Prep for Eating Later

  1. Refrigerate.

Eating Later

  1. Microwave and eat.
