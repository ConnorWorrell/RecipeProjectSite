White Butter Sauce (Beurre Blanc)

Note: Makes approximately 1 cup.

Ingredients

IngredientStandardStandard UnitWeight 1Unit 1Weight 2Unit 2
Vinegar White Wine2.5Tbsp
Wine Dry White2.5Tbsp
Shallots (Minced)1Tbsp
Butter Unsalted18Tbsp254g9oz
Salt.5Tspn
White Pepper Ground.125Tspn

Objects

Object(Optional)
SaucepanNo

Instructions

  1. Cut butter into 1 Tbsp (14g) chunks
  2. Combine vinegar, wine, shallots, 2 butter chunks (2Tbsp, 28g), boil until size is ~1.5 Tbsp
  3. Whisk butter into sauce 1 at a time, no boiling.
  4. Strain.

Eating Now

  1. Keep warm.

Prep for Eating Later

Not recommended.

  1. Chill

Eating Later

  1. Mix into hot liquid (wine, meat drippings or cream)
