Hungarian Mushroom Soup

Ingredients

IngredientStandardStandard UnitWeight 1Unit 1Weight 2Unit 2
Butter (Unsalted)2Tbsp
Onion2Cups
Mushrooms1.5lb
Dill4Tspn
Paprika (Sweet)1Tbsp
Soy Sauce1Tbsp
Broth (Chicken)2Cups
Milk1Cups
Flour (All Purpose)3Tbsp
Tomato (Large)0.5Tomatoes
Pepper (Hungarian Wax) Pepper (Jalapeno)0.5Peppers
Sour Cream0.5Cups
Salt
Pepper

Objects

Object(Optional)
Large pot

Instructions

  1. Chop onions.
  2. Melt butter in pod, add onions, cook 5 mins.
  3. Add mushrooms, cook 5 mins.
  4. Add dill, paprika, soy sauce, broth, change heat to low, simmer 15 mins.
  5. Seperatly mix milk and flour, add to soup.
  6. Cut tomato and pepper in half, add to soup, simmer 15 mins.
  7. Mix sour cream into soup, simmer 10 mins.
  8. Remove tomato and pepper halves.
